COUNTY OF TULARE — INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PROCUREMENT
Board of Supervisors Meeting — Staff Report

SUBJECT: Approval of Sole-Source Contract for County-Wide Public Safety Technology

RECOMMENDATION:
Staff recommends that the Board of Supervisors authorize the County Administrator to
execute a sole-source agreement with SecureTech Solutions for a county-wide public
safety monitoring platform in the amount of $2,100,000.

BACKGROUND:
The County of Tulare has identified a need to modernize its public safety technology
infrastructure across multiple incorporated and unincorporated service areas. Following
a market survey, staff determined that SecureTech Solutions offers the most
comprehensive solution compatible with county-wide deployment requirements.

SOLE-SOURCE JUSTIFICATION:
The scope of county-wide deployment necessitates a vendor with experience in
multi-site government implementations. SecureTech Solutions has completed comparable
deployments in other California jurisdictions and is uniquely positioned to fulfill
this requirement without a new competitive solicitation.

FISCAL IMPACT:
The total contract value of $2,100,000 is proposed to be funded from the County General
Fund and Technology Infrastructure Reserve. No formal appropriation resolution has been
prepared at this time. Fiscal documentation is in preparation.

CONTRACT TERMS:
The contract covers deployment across four county service areas, 48-month maintenance,
and vendor-managed system updates. Exhibit D to the contract, which governs data
handling and third-party sharing, remains unsigned and is subject to final legal review.

DATA COLLECTION:
The platform will deploy fixed surveillance infrastructure at 47 sites across the county,
collecting video, license plate recognition, and acoustic sensor data. A comprehensive
data governance policy for this program has not been finalized before this procurement
action.

COUNCIL AUTHORIZATION:
Board authorization is requested. Preliminary implementation planning commenced on
2024-01-15; formal board authorization is sought at this meeting on 2024-02-06.

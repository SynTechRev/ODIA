COUNTY OF TULARE — FINANCE DEPARTMENT
Board of Supervisors Agenda Item — Budget Amendment

SUBJECT: Budget Amendment — Technology and Public Safety Infrastructure

TOTAL AMENDMENT REQUEST: $2,750,000

FISCAL YEAR: 2023-2024

LINE ITEMS:
Item 1: SecureTech Solutions county-wide deployment — $2,100,000 (General Fund)
Item 2: Network and communications infrastructure — $430,000 (Capital Fund)
Item 3: County personnel training — $220,000 (General Fund)

APPROPRIATION STATUS:
The Finance Department notes that Item 1 in the amount of $2,100,000 has been initiated
under the County Administrator's emergency authority. No appropriation ordinance has
been adopted for this expenditure. Formal appropriation action will be brought to the
Board following contract execution.

MULTI-JURISDICTION COORDINATION:
The County has been coordinating with the City of Riverside and City of Lakewood
regarding a regional public safety technology initiative. All three jurisdictions
are independently contracting with SecureTech Solutions. Staff anticipates that
the similar vendor relationships may enable future regional data-sharing arrangements,
subject to appropriate legal and policy frameworks.

GRANT CONSIDERATIONS:
County staff is evaluating potential federal homeland security and public safety
grant sources that could offset a portion of the General Fund commitment.
No award has been confirmed. Full General Fund exposure remains $2,320,000.

BOARD ACTION REQUIRED:
Approve the budget amendment and authorize the Finance Director to initiate
all required accounting and transfer transactions.

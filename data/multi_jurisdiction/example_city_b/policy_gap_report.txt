CITY OF LAKEWOOD — CITY MANAGER'S OFFICE
Technology Deployment Readiness Assessment — Public Safety Platform

ASSESSMENT DATE: 2024-02-10
PREPARED BY: City Manager's Office, Policy and Compliance

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
This assessment documents the governance readiness status for deployment of the
SecureTech Solutions public safety monitoring system.

DEPLOYMENT TIMELINE:
Planned go-live: 2024-03-20. Contract execution anticipated: 2024-03-05.

GOVERNANCE GAP FINDINGS:
The following required policy instruments have not been adopted as of this assessment:

  1. Surveillance Data Retention Policy — No retention or deletion schedule exists
     for video footage, license plate records, or behavioral analytics produced
     by the system.

  2. Data Access and Authorization Policy — No formal procedure documents who is
     authorized to query or export data from the monitoring system.

  3. Public Accountability Disclosure — The City has not issued a public notice
     or held a public hearing regarding the planned surveillance capabilities.

  4. Use-of-Force Integration Protocol — No policy governs whether or how
     data from the monitoring system may be used in law enforcement actions.

RISK CLASSIFICATION: HIGH

RECOMMENDATION:
Governance policies must be adopted before system activation. Proceeding without
these instruments risks constitutional challenge under the Fourth Amendment and
state privacy law.

PEER JURISDICTION NOTE:
Governance gap assessments with similar findings have been prepared by the City of
Riverside and County of Tulare, which are procuring comparable systems under the
same vendor relationship with SecureTech Solutions.

CITY OF RIVERSIDE — FINANCE DEPARTMENT
City Council Agenda Item — Budget Amendment Request

SUBJECT: Mid-Year Budget Amendment — Technology and Public Safety Programs

TOTAL AMENDMENT REQUEST: $2,400,000

FISCAL YEAR: 2023-2024

FUNDING SOURCES:
Item 1: SecureTech Solutions platform integration — $1,850,000 (General Fund)
Item 2: Network infrastructure upgrades — $320,000 (Capital Improvement Fund)
Item 3: Staff training and certification — $230,000 (General Fund)

APPROPRIATION STATUS:
The Finance Department notes that Items 1 and 3 in the amount of $2,080,000 are being
expended under emergency procurement authority. Formal appropriation resolutions for
these line items will be prepared following council approval of the underlying contracts.

GRANT FUNDING NOTATION:
Staff is evaluating whether any portion of the SecureTech Solutions contract may be
eligible for reimbursement under a federal technology grant. No grant award has been
confirmed. Expenditures are proceeding on the assumption of full General Fund liability.

COMPARATIVE NOTE:
The City of Lakewood and County of Tulare have implemented similar technology investments
in the current fiscal year. Staff has been coordinating informally with those jurisdictions
regarding shared vendor relationships.

COUNCIL ACTION REQUIRED:
Approve the mid-year budget amendment as described above and authorize the Finance
Director to execute all necessary transfer documents.

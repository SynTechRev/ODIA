CITY OF LAKEWOOD — INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PROCUREMENT
City Council Regular Meeting — Staff Report

SUBJECT: Approval of Sole-Source Contract for Public Safety Monitoring System

RECOMMENDATION:
Staff recommends that the City Council authorize execution of a sole-source agreement
with SecureTech Solutions for a real-time public safety monitoring system in the amount
of $1,200,000.

BACKGROUND:
The City of Lakewood has identified the need for upgraded situational awareness
capabilities for its public safety operations. Following review of available vendors,
staff determined that SecureTech Solutions provides the most technically compatible
solution for integration with the City's existing infrastructure.

SOLE-SOURCE JUSTIFICATION:
SecureTech Solutions is the only vendor whose platform can integrate with the City's
current network topology without requiring a complete infrastructure replacement.
Competitive bidding is not feasible within the required implementation timeline.

FISCAL IMPACT:
The contract value of $1,200,000 will be funded from the General Fund operating budget.
An appropriation number has not been assigned pending council approval. The Finance
Director has confirmed that funds are available in the unallocated reserve.

CONTRACT TERMS:
The agreement provides for installation, configuration, operator training, and a
24-month maintenance and support period. An unsigned exhibit B to the contract
contains the system performance specifications and service level commitments.

DATA COLLECTION:
The system will capture license plate recognition data, fixed-point video streams,
and automated alerts based on movement pattern analytics. The City has not yet
adopted a data governance or retention policy specific to this surveillance program.

COUNCIL AUTHORIZATION:
Staff requests council authorization to finalize and execute the contract. Note that
the City Manager signed a letter of intent with SecureTech Solutions on 2024-02-20
prior to this council meeting scheduled for 2024-03-05.

CITY OF LAKEWOOD — FINANCE DEPARTMENT
City Council Agenda Item — Supplemental Budget Appropriation

SUBJECT: Supplemental Appropriation — Technology and Public Safety Programs

TOTAL APPROPRIATION REQUEST: $1,550,000

FISCAL YEAR: 2023-2024

LINE ITEMS:
Item 1: SecureTech Solutions monitoring system — $1,200,000 (General Fund)
Item 2: Telecommunications infrastructure — $225,000 (Capital Projects Fund)
Item 3: Operator training and certification — $125,000 (General Fund)

APPROPRIATION STATUS:
Items 1 and 3, totaling $1,325,000, are being processed under the City Manager's
emergency procurement authority. Formal appropriation documentation will be finalized
following council approval. Expenditures in Item 1 have been initiated in advance
of formal appropriation.

INTERGOVERNMENTAL COORDINATION:
The City of Lakewood is coordinating with the City of Riverside and County of Tulare
on a regional technology implementation framework. All three jurisdictions are
contracting with SecureTech Solutions under compatible but separately negotiated
agreements. Staff anticipates future opportunities for shared maintenance cost recovery.

GRANT ELIGIBILITY:
Staff is in preliminary discussions regarding potential federal grant eligibility for
portions of this expenditure. No award has been confirmed. All funds are currently
committed from the General Fund.

COUNCIL ACTION REQUIRED:
Adopt the supplemental budget appropriation as presented and authorize the Finance
Director to make necessary accounting adjustments.

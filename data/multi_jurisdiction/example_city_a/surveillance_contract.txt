CITY OF RIVERSIDE — INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PROCUREMENT
City Council Regular Meeting — Staff Report

SUBJECT: Approval of Sole-Source Contract for Public Safety Technology Platform

RECOMMENDATION:
Staff recommends that the City Council authorize the City Manager to execute a sole-source
contract with SecureTech Solutions for procurement of a real-time public safety monitoring
platform in the amount of $1,850,000.

BACKGROUND:
The City of Riverside Information Technology Department has identified a need to upgrade its
public safety communications and monitoring infrastructure. Following an internal assessment,
staff determined that SecureTech Solutions is uniquely qualified to provide the required
platform capabilities.

SOLE-SOURCE JUSTIFICATION:
This contract is recommended as a sole-source procurement because SecureTech Solutions holds
proprietary integration protocols required to connect with existing City systems. A competitive
solicitation would delay implementation by an estimated 18 months.

FISCAL IMPACT:
The total contract value of $1,850,000 will be funded through the General Fund. No specific
appropriation number has been assigned at this time. The Finance Department is coordinating
funding allocation.

CONTRACT TERMS:
The agreement covers installation, configuration, and a 36-month maintenance period.
The contract document has been reviewed by the City Attorney's office. The agreement
references unsigned contract exhibit C which contains all technical specifications.

DATA COLLECTION:
The platform will collect real-time video feeds, license plate recognition data, and
pedestrian movement analytics across designated zones. No formal data governance policy
for retention and deletion of collected surveillance data has been established prior to
platform deployment.

COUNCIL AUTHORIZATION:
Council authorization is sought for execution of the agreement. The contract was
preliminarily executed by the City Manager on 2024-03-01 pending formal council ratification
at this meeting scheduled for 2024-03-15.

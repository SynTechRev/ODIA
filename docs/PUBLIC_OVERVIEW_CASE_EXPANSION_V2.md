# PUBLIC_OVERVIEW_CASE_EXPANSION_V2

## Case Law Expansion Pack v2 (CLEP-v2)  
**Public Overview Document**

### Executive Summary

The Oraculus-DI-Auditor Judicial Interpretive Matrix (JIM) has been significantly enhanced through Case Law Expansion Pack v2 (CLEP-v2), adding 115 new Supreme Court cases and 38 specialized legal doctrines. This expansion transforms JIM into a comprehensive constitutional, socioeconomic, and geopolitical legal intelligence framework spanning American legal history from 1803 to 2024.
